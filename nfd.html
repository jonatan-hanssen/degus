Terms and conditions for NFDs
  • A non fungible degus, hereby referred to as an NFD, represents proof of ownership of a theoretical degus in the chilean wilderness. It does not necessarily represent any specific degus, or any degus at all
  • The Degus Corporation offers no guarantee that the degus hypothetically represented by the NFD is still alive, or ever was alive
  • If the degus represented by a specific NFD ever leaves the chilean wilderness, for example by venturing into the cites of Iquique or Antofagasta, or by crossing the long Argentine-Chilean border into the Argentinian wilderness, the NFD will immediately be destroyed through the process of "degus termination". The degus however, will be fine
  • The Degus Corporation does not offer tours of the chilean wilderness, or offer any way to contact the degus, or interact with the degus in any way
  • If a degus wishes to expropriate the NFD that represents it, it may do so by contacting the Degus Corporation
  • It is possible for several NFDs to represent a single degus, or for several deguses to represent a single NFD (degus duplication), but this is very unlikely and should be avoided
